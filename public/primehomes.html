About Primehomes

Excellence in Real Estate Development

Leading Real Estate Excellence

Primehomes Real Estate Development, Inc. has been active in the local business sector as a comprehensive real estate development enterprise, having been awarded several multi-billion-peso landmark projects in very prime locations that are set to shape the future of our nation towards growth, progress and sustainability.

  • More Than An Advocate of Green Architecture and Responsible Design
  • We take pride in creating purposeful communities that become the catalyst for subsequent urban development leaving a legacy of collaboration between man and nature.
  • ISO Certified Excellence in Quality Standards and Customer Satisfaction

Our ISO certification stands as a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality requirements set by international organizations for global standardization. This certification provides concrete assurance to our clients that we prioritize the customer journey, including service excellence and complete satisfaction.

What Drives Us Forward

Love of Nature

Nothing rejuvenates the spirit and provides respite like nature. Thus, we are committed to keeping our developments predominantly open and green lush areas, allowing residents to expand their living spaces beyond the confines of their units.

Excellence

In building, designing, after-sales service, property management - we are driven to deliver beyond expectations.

Future Forward

We are dedicated to sustainability in our developments to promote eco-conscious living. And we capitalize on smart technology for efficiency and convenience.

Quality of Life

We look at our residents not as customers but as members of our community. We maintain and improve our facilities & services, always with the goal of promoting a better quality of life.
